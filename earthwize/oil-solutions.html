Enviroshore Service 01

Sludge & Lagoon Remediation

With a holistic approach to environmentally friendly removal and recovery of waste oil, Enviroshore is fully equipped to remove, transport, and safely recycle slop/sludge waste oil through a specialised treatment facility into a usable product — reducing carbon emissions.

We have developed a full cradle-to-grave solution for sludge ponds and lagoons whereby hydrocarbons are recovered and soil and groundwater is remediated with minimal waste needing to go to costly hazardous landfills.

The SLOPO® System from Oreco

Efficient and innovative slop oil treatment using centrifugal separator technology. Converts a wide range of slop oils into valuable products. HAZOP certified, plug-and-play containerised units available in standard configurations for any capacity requirement.

Enviroshore Service 02

Waste Oil Processing Facilities

Enviroshore has established state-of-the-art mobile waste oil processing plants at the Port of Motola in Mozambique and Cato Ridge in KwaZulu-Natal. These plants produce up to 10,000 tonnes of finished product per month — both high-grade heavy fuel oil and light fuel oil meeting international standards.

The plants operate as a self-contained "Recovery Centre": hydrocarbon oils collected by vacuum/road tankers, stored in bulk, processed on-site. All waste water is treated using enzyme and microbial technology. Vapour recovery systems ensure zero atmospheric release.

  • Oil phase → Oil Recovery system
  • Solids phase → Thermal Oil System
  • Water phase → Waste Water Treatment
  • Vapour → Recovery and recapture

Surf Cleaner: Oil Recovery Units

EarthWize is the official agent for Surf Cleaner units in South Africa — integrating this Swedish technology leader's advanced oil recovery solutions into our service offering for oil and gas, shipping, and industrial sectors.

The Surf Cleaner Unit is a highly efficient, mobile oil recovery system that can recover up to 98.5% of oil from water — ideal for oil spill remediation, marine operations, and industrial water treatment.
