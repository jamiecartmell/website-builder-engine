Service 01

Tank Cleaning Solutions

At EarthWize, we understand that the value of storage space is critical — just as essential as the product being stored. Our innovative approach to tank cleaning leverages specialised technology to ensure both safety and efficiency.

As the industry evolves, with refineries shifting to terminals for the importation of finished products, the need for cleaning crude oil tanks to handle a diverse range of products has never been greater.

No Man Entry Tank Cleaning System

  • 360° cleaning with 3 rotating, orientable nozzles — adjustable 8–200 bar
  • Compact skid-mounted system with telescopic arm
  • De-greasing, wash, vacuum, and video inspection simultaneously
  • Manhole: Ø 18"–450mm | L 47"–1200mm | H 14"–360mm
  • ATEX Rated — safely controlled from a distance

Service 03

Soil & Groundwater Remediation

EarthWize has more than 30 years of experience in soil and groundwater remediation around the world. Our specialised technology and unique products have passed Dutch, German, and South African standards.

Our Bio-Remediation products harness natural processes by promoting the growth of micro-organisms that degrade hydrocarbon contaminants, converting them into harmless by-products (H₂O, CO₂). It is more cost-effective to rehabilitate the soil and groundwater in-situ than removing contamination off-site.

EarthWize has the ability to rehabilitate soil and groundwater to its natural state in-situ, eliminating the exorbitant cost of transporting the problem from one area to another.

  • In-situ bio-remediation — no excavation required
  • Patented formulations for hydrocarbon degradation
  • Dutch, German, and South African standard compliance
  • Financial recovery opportunities for recovered hydrocarbons
Service 04

Waste Oil Recovery

EarthWize specialises in the efficient recovery of waste oils using advanced pumps and specialised equipment. We collect used oils from industrial, automotive, and other sources, and transport them to our processing facilities where waste oil is re-processed into valuable energy.

Economic Benefits

  • Reduces need for new oils/fuels
  • Revenue generation potential
  • Resource optimisation

Environmental Benefits

  • Prevents landfill and waterway pollution
  • Reduces carbon footprint
  • Circular economy support

The Kraker Unit — our state-of-the-art mobile distillation unit — filters and recovers contaminated fuels by separating unwanted impurities and commingled fuels, restoring them to optimal quality.
