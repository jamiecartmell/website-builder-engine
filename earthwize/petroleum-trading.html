Global Petroleum Product Trading

EarthWize operates across the full petroleum value chain — from responsible liquid product transfers and fuel filtration to international trading partnerships with Select Energy AG Zug and Dominion Environmental.

Overview

Responsible Petroleum Operations

EarthWize provides responsible fuel and oil trading, environmental hydrocarbon cleaning, and bio-remediation products. Our petroleum trading operations are underpinned by a deep understanding of product quality, logistics, and environmental compliance.

As a vertically integrated group, we connect recovery operations at our Enviroshore facilities — converting waste hydrocarbon material into tradeable fuel products — with responsible downstream distribution to clients across South Africa and the region.

  • Specialised liquid product transfers — minimising waste
  • Fuel quality recovery and restoration via Kraker Unit
  • Responsible petroleum product distribution
  • Integration with Enviroshore waste oil processing
  • Environmental compliance at every stage
