EarthWize Environmental Products

A proprietary range of South African-developed environmental products for soil treatment, hydrocarbon remediation, spill containment, and water purification — proven in the field across petroleum, mining, and industrial sectors.

Product Range

Our Environmental Product Suite

All EarthWize environmental products are designed to work with nature, not against it — harnessing biological, chemical, and mineral processes to solve real environmental challenges.

Product 01

MST — Microbe Soil Treatment

MST is EarthWize's flagship bio-remediation product — a concentrated blend of naturally occurring micro-organisms specifically selected for their ability to degrade hydrocarbon contaminants in soil and groundwater.

MST works in-situ, eliminating the need to excavate and transport contaminated soil. The micro-organisms convert hydrocarbons into harmless by-products (CO₂ and H₂O), restoring soil to its natural state while meeting Dutch, German, and South African standards.

Key Benefits

  • In-situ treatment — no excavation required
  • Degrades petroleum hydrocarbons, PAHs, and BTEX compounds
  • Meets international remediation standards
  • Cost-effective versus conventional dig-and-dump methods
  • No secondary waste generation
Bio-RemediationIn-SituSA Standards
Product 02

Bio Treat — Biological Water Treatment

Bio Treat is a liquid biological treatment designed for wastewater, sewage lagoons, and effluent streams. It contains a blend of selected bacterial strains that break down organic matter, reduce BOD/COD levels, and control odour.

Ideal for use in industrial effluent plants, municipal wastewater, and holding lagoons — Bio Treat is non-toxic, non-pathogenic, and environmentally safe.

  • Reduces BOD and COD levels significantly
  • Controls odour in lagoons and effluent systems
  • Non-toxic, non-pathogenic bacterial blend
  • Safe for use in active treatment systems
  • Suitable for industrial, mining, and municipal applications
WastewaterOdour ControlBiological
Product 03

SpillSorb — Organic Spill Absorbent

SpillSorb is the number-one choice for treating oil and chemical spills. Its unique 'barrel' cell structure encapsulates pollutants not found in other products. SpillSorb is 100% organic, non-toxic, biodegradable, and self-generating in its natural state.

Key Benefits

  • 100% organic and non-toxic
  • Biodegradable — no environmental harm
  • Eliminates up to 90% of VOCs and LELs
  • Powerful wicking action
  • Lightweight and easy to apply

Applications

  • Oil and chemical spills
  • Petroleum product spills
  • Industrial floor and surface spills
  • Marine and coastal spill containment
  • Emergency response kits
OrganicNon-ToxicBiodegradable
Product 04

Xo Sorb — Advanced Hydrocarbon Absorbent

Xo Sorb is an advanced mineral-based absorbent engineered specifically for heavy hydrocarbon spills including crude oil, heavy fuel oil, and thick sludge. Its granular structure provides superior absorption capacity for viscous materials that standard absorbents cannot handle effectively.

  • High absorption capacity for viscous hydrocarbons
  • Effective on crude oil, HFO, and heavy sludge
  • Mineral-based — chemically inert and stable
  • Suitable for refinery and terminal environments
  • Easy disposal and low secondary waste
Heavy HydrocarbonsMineral-Based
Product 05

Bio Carb — Activated Carbon Water Purification

Bio Carb is an activated carbon-based water purification product designed to adsorb dissolved hydrocarbons, heavy metals, and organic compounds from water streams. Used in polishing stages of water treatment, Bio Carb delivers final-stage purification to meet discharge and reuse standards.

  • Adsorbs dissolved hydrocarbons and organic compounds
  • Removes heavy metals from industrial water streams
  • Used in polishing and final-stage water treatment
  • Meets discharge standards for industrial and municipal use
  • Available in granular and pellet forms
Water PurificationActivated Carbon
Product 06

OilGone TC — Industrial Tank Cleaner

OilGone TC (Tank Cleaner) is a concentrated industrial cleaner used to remove residues and heavy organic deposits from tanks, tank farms, tank cars, and tank containers. Effective on natural oil, synthetic oil, crude oil, heavy fuel oils, and lubricant products.

OilGone TC contains surface-active ingredients, is pH neutral, and exhibits excellent wetting and cleaning properties. It can be applied through tank cleaning equipment, spray nozzles, and spray injectors.

Advantages Over Standard Cleaners

  • Reduced water usage — lower wastewater disposal costs
  • pH neutral — safe on all tank materials
  • Effective on both organic and inorganic water-soluble residues
  • Compatible with No Man Entry tank cleaning systems
