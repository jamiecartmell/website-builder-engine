About EarthWize

A proudly South African environmental group delivering world-class solutions since the 1990s — built on expertise, integrity, and a deep commitment to the planet.

Executive Summary

Who We Are

EarthWize Environmental is a leading, proudly South African B-BBEE Level 2 contributor, headquartered in Kloof, Durban, KwaZulu-Natal. We specialise in innovative environmental solutions serving the petroleum, shipping, mining, and government sectors.

Our commitment is to deliver cutting-edge services that address environmental challenges and promote sustainability. As a vertically integrated company, we specialise in the proper treatment, recycling, and disposal of both hazardous and non-hazardous hydrocarbon waste.

Through strategic international partnerships, EarthWize ensures world-class, sustainable solutions across South Africa, Mozambique, and beyond — positioning us as a leader in supporting the continent's industrial sector with innovative, environmentally responsible solutions.

Our Foundation

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To deliver cutting-edge, sustainable environmental services that protect ecosystems, support industry compliance, and convert environmental liabilities into valuable assets — while advancing South Africa's industrial sector responsibly.

Our Vision

To become the principal partner of choice in Africa for all areas of rehabilitation, on-site storage clean-up, soil remediation, and oil recovery — incorporating world-class waste oil processing and environmental technology.

Our Values

  • Safety first — zero harm to people and planet
  • Environmental stewardship in every operation
  • Innovation through international partnerships
  • Transformation — proudly B-BBEE Level 2
  • Integrity and transparency with all stakeholders
B-BBEE Level 2

Proudly South African

EarthWize Environmental is a proud Broad-Based Black Economic Empowerment (B-BBEE) Level 2 contributor — one of the highest ratings attainable. This reflects our deep commitment to South Africa's transformation agenda and to building an inclusive, sustainable economy.

Headquartered in Kloof, Durban, we are embedded in the KwaZulu-Natal industrial landscape, with operations extending to Cato Ridge, Mozambique, and internationally through our global partner network.
