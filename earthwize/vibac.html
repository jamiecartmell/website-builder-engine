Vibac — 70% Alcohol Based Sanitizer

A professional-grade, broad-spectrum alcohol sanitizer for industrial, commercial, and healthcare environments. Effective against bacteria, viruses, and fungi — compliant with international hygiene standards.

Product Overview

Vibac — Professional Hygiene Solution

Vibac is EarthWize's 70% isopropyl alcohol-based sanitizer, formulated for professional use across industrial, commercial, healthcare, and food-processing environments. Its 70% alcohol concentration is the scientifically proven optimal ratio for maximum antimicrobial efficacy.

The 70% alcohol concentration — rather than higher concentrations — is more effective because it denatures proteins of bacteria and viruses while allowing sufficient contact time for full penetration of the cell membrane. Vibac is fast-drying, leaves no residue, and is safe for use on skin and most hard surfaces.

Industrial Applications

Ideal for industrial facilities including refineries, processing plants, laboratories, and manufacturing environments where hygiene compliance is critical and regular sanitation of surfaces, equipment, and hands is required.

  • Equipment surface sanitation
  • Control panels and touchpoints
  • Personal hand hygiene stations

Healthcare & Food Grade

Suitable for healthcare facilities and food processing environments. Meets stringent hygiene requirements without toxic residue — safe for incidental food contact surfaces when properly applied and allowed to dry.

  • Hospital and clinic sanitation
  • Food processing surface treatment
  • Laboratory and cleanroom use

Commercial & Office

Widely used in office environments, retail, hospitality, and transportation sectors. Available in a range of formats — individual sachets, pump dispensers, bulk containers, and wall-mount units for high-traffic areas.

  • Desk, keyboard, and phone surfaces
  • Reception and high-traffic sanitation points
  • Transport and logistics vehicles

Packaging Options
