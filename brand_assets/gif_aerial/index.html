What is aerial yoga

"Suspended in silk,
you'll find a new freedom
in your practice."

Aerial yoga uses a soft fabric hammock suspended from the ceiling to support your body weight. It allows deeper stretches, full spinal decompression, and movements that simply aren't possible on a mat.

Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced yogi looking for something new — the silk meets you exactly where you are.

Spinal decompression & joint relief
Core strength without strain
Mindfulness through movement
Genuinely playful & joyful
