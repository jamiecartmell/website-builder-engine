Common Questions

Before your first class.

Do I need any experience?
Not at all. The Beginners class is designed for complete newcomers — no flexibility, fitness level or prior yoga experience required. Gif will guide you through everything from the very beginning.
What should I wear?
Fitted clothing that covers your armpits and the backs of your knees. The silk can pinch loose fabric, so form-fitting activewear works best. Avoid zips or buckles that might catch.
Is it safe?
Yes. Gif guides every student through the hammock carefully. Classes are deliberately small — max 10 students — so she can give proper attention to every person in the room.
How high off the ground will I be?
Not very. The hammock is set low enough that you're never far from the floor — especially in the beginner class. You'll always feel grounded, even when you're in the air.
Can I come if I'm not fit?
Absolutely. Aerial yoga builds fitness — it doesn't require it. The beginner class is designed to ease you in gently, building strength and coordination over time at your own pace.
How do I pay?
Gif will confirm the current rates and payment details when she gets back to you. Cash, Revolut and bank transfer are all accepted. There's no online payment required.
How do I know which class is right for me?
If you're unsure, just ask. Send Gif a message, tell her where you're starting from, and she'll point you in the right direction honestly. Almost everyone starts with the Beginners class and that's exactly the right place to be.

Ready to reserve your spot?

Drop Gif a message. She'll get back to you within 24 hours with everything you need — which class to start with, what to bring, and how to find the studio.
