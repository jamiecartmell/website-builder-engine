Classes

Three classes.
One journey.

Each class is built for a different stage of the journey — all of them taught by Gif in Westport, with small groups of up to 10 students and a warm, unhurried pace. Whether you've never set foot in a hammock or you're ready to go deeper, there's a class for where you are right now.

01
Beginner No experience needed

Aerial Yoga
for Beginners

Tuesday
7:30 – 8:30 pm

If you've ever been curious about aerial yoga but weren't sure where to start — this is it. Gif designed this class to remove every barrier. No experience, no special fitness level, no particular flexibility required. Just a willingness to try something different.

You'll learn how to use the hammock safely, move through the foundational poses, and begin to build the coordination and core strength that underpins everything else. Most students are surprised by how quickly they settle in — and how good it feels.

Hammock safety & setup
Foundational poses & coordination
Core strength basics
Max 10 students — personal attention guaranteed

02
Improver Min. 4 beginner classes

Improver
Aerial

Wednesday
7:30 – 8:30 pm

You've found your footing. Now it's time to go further. The Improver class is for students who've moved through the beginner foundations and are ready to bring more intention to their practice.

Classes focus on technical refinement — the quality of movement, the engagement of the right muscles, the transitions between poses that start to feel seamless rather than effortful. If you're not sure whether you're ready, just ask Gif. She'll tell you honestly.

Technical refinement & muscle engagement
Seamless transitions between poses
Progressive core strength
Max 10 students — personal attention guaranteed

03
All levels welcome

Aerial
Hammock

Tuesday
6:30 – 7:30 pm

This is aerial yoga at its most expressive. The Aerial Hammock class brings together movement, rhythm and the unique sensation of being held by the silk — and invites you to explore what's possible within that.

It's the class where the practice starts to feel less like exercise and more like something else entirely. Something that's harder to put into words, but that students come back to week after week.

Fluid transitions & rhythmic sequences
Creative & expressive movement
Calming, flowing practice
Max 10 students — personal attention guaranteed
