My Story

"A circus came to my village when I was five. I've been chasing that feeling ever since."

Gif — Founder & Teacher
Where it all began

Krabi, Thailand.
1997.

Gif grew up in the countryside of Krabi, Thailand, where at five years old a travelling circus changed everything. The sight of performers suspended in the air — moving with grace, strength and pure joy — planted a seed that would take over two decades to bloom.

That image never left her. A child sitting in the grass, watching someone fly.

The Journey

From the tropics
to the Wild Atlantic.

At 28, she returned to that childhood dream and began her aerial yoga training in earnest. Three years ago, she made the journey from Thailand to County Mayo — exchanging tropical coastlines for the wild Atlantic, and bringing her practice with her.

Westport, it turns out, was exactly the right place for it.

Teaching in Westport

Small classes.
Real care.

For the past two years, Gif has been teaching aerial yoga in Westport. Her classes are small by design — because she believes the practice works best when there's real care in the room.

Every student's growth matters to her, whether it's their first tentative time in the hammock or their first time holding a pose they didn't think was possible.

Max 10 students per class
The Practice

Something you return to, week after week.

Gif's approach is gentle, patient, and quietly joyful. She doesn't teach aerial yoga as a performance — she teaches it as a practice. Something that gives back a little more each time.

The studio is welcoming by design. Nobody is expected to be graceful on day one. Nobody is pushed before they're ready. The pace is yours.

"Every class, every student, every moment of movement on this island fills my heart. I'm so glad you found your way here."

— Gif
