Our Story

Timeless Character,
Warm Welcome

Nestled in the heart of Westport, The ClockHouse Cafe is rooted in the feeling of time well spent — unhurried mornings, the scent of fresh coffee, and craftsmanship in every cup.

We believe in the old virtues: sourcing locally, baking daily, and serving with genuine care. Every detail, from the hand-laminated pastries to the carefully pulled espresso, reflects a commitment to artisan quality.

  • Heritage & timelessness — classic forms, never trendy
  • Warmth & hospitality — an inviting space, a generous spirit
  • Crafted quality — artisan detail in every plate and cup
  • Local & authentic — rooted in Westport and community
